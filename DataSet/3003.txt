The Rajalakshmi Engineering College is an autonomous private engineering college that was established in the year 1997. The institute was started under the supervision of the Rajalakshmi Educational Trust and has been approved by the AICTE. The institute is affiliated to the Anna University.

The institute is ISO 9001:2008 certified and was awarded the International Organization for Standardization 9002 certificate. It has been accredited Grade A by the NAAC. All the engineering courses are recognised and accredited by the Institution of Engineers (IEI). The institute offers BE in Aeronautical Engineering, Automobile Engineering, Bio-Medical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Mechatronics and Bio-Technology. MTech and MBA courses are also offered by the institute.
